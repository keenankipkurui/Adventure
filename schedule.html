A Typical Day

Each Day schedule.
Time Activity Description
7 a.m Breakfast You will be served with enough healthy breakfast making sure you are all ready for the day
7:30 a.m Devotions Devotions are a time for the group to get ready for the day ahead. Devotions can be anything from the reading of a favorite poem or story to the leading of an icebreaker
8 a.m -4 p.m Enjoying time!! The visitors will be taken to various tourist sites and help them make the day the most memorable one. Other activities together with the site seeing will also be carried out at this time like playing beach volleyball, water surfing, swimming an so on.
6 p.m Dinner Dinner with a variety of food stuffs will be served. Some beach parties are organised at this time of the day.
7:30 p.m Evening activity The evening activities focus on building a sense of community within the group , Knowing the different cultures from different community, socialising with the rest of the visitors and a perfect time to relax-free from the day to day life.(thinking outside the box).
